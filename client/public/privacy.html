Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-12

RogueSim respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. If you have questions, contact us at uplink@roguesim.com.

Information We Collect

  • Account details you provide (e.g., email, username).
  • Gameplay and progress necessary to provide core features.
  • Diagnostics and logs to ensure service reliability and security.

How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve RogueSim features.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to abuse or technical issues.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., email, payments) strictly to operate the service, under contractual obligations and applicable law.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we strive to protect your data.

Your Choices

  • Access, update, or delete certain profile information in-app.
  • Request data export or deletion by contacting support.
  • Control cookies via browser settings.

Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined here, unless longer retention is required by law.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated through the app or via email when appropriate.
